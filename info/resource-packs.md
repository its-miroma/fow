# Resource Packs

FO enables some mod-provided resource packs, and it bundles a few other resource packs for better experience and clarity:

* [**Chat Reporting Helper**](https://github.com/Fabulously-Optimized/fabulously-optimized/tree/main/Resource%20Packs/Chat%20Reporting%20Helper): explains the player reporting features in a clearer and less technical way, by changing tooltips and icons from both vanilla and No Chat Reports. Read more about [FO's stance on player reporting](mods/player-reporting.md)
* [**Fast Better Grass**](https://github.com/Fabulously-Optimized/fabulously-optimized/tree/main/Resource%20Packs/Fast%20Better%20Grass): imitates OptiFine's "Better Grass" fast mode. Read more about [OptiFine features in FO](../about/optifine.md)
* [**Mod Menu Helper**](https://github.com/Fabulously-Optimized/fabulously-optimized/tree/main/Resource%20Packs/Mod%20Menu%20Helper): improves the Mod Menu by explaining what each mod does in a consistent and clear way. It also adds icons showing configurable mods:
  * If `🛠️` or `✏️` is blue, you can configure the mod by clicking the config button ![config](https://i.ibb.co/j35cBtn/image.png)
  * If you can't see `🛠️` or `✏️`, then MMH is disabled
    1. Click on `Done`
    2. Click on `Options...`
    3. Click on `Resource Packs...`
    4. Click on `⏵` next to **Mod Menu Helper.zip**
    5. Click on `Done`
* [**Translations for Sodium**](https://github.com/Madis0/sodium-fabric-translations): implements unofficial translations for the Sodium mod. Read more about [localization in Sodium natively](https://github.com/CaffeineMC/sodium-fabric/issues/2304)

{% hint style="success" %}
The resource packs use minimal resources, they are highly compatible, 100% open-source, and they can be [translated to your language](../about/contribute/translate.md).
{% endhint %}
