# Mod Menu Helper

Mod Menu Helper improves the Mod Menu by explaining what each mod does in a consistent and clear way. MMH is exclusive to FO.

In the Mod Menu, you can read the descriptions of the mods to see what you can customize:

* If `🛠️` or `✏️` is blue, you can configure the mod by clicking the config button ![config](https://i.ibb.co/j35cBtn/image.png)
* If you can't see `🛠️` or `✏️`, then MMH is disabled
   1. Click on `Done`
   2. Click on `Options...`
   3. Click on `Resource Packs...`
   4. Click on `⏵` next to **Mod Menu Helper.zip**
   5. Click on `Done`

You can find [MMH on GitHub](https://github.com/Fabulously-Optimized/fabulously-optimized/tree/main/Resource%20Packs/Mod%20Menu%20Helper).
